About us

We are a couple of protectors who, loving animals so much, decided to dedicate our life to them, so in 1996, after thinking very carefully, we decided to be more active in protecting animals.

With the money we had collected in more than 10 years of our computer technician jobs (she) and carpenter (he), we decided to buy land and build a sanctuary for animals.

Every day, after feeding the animals, we go out with our van through the streets of the city looking for injured or abandoned animals.

After caring, feeding and treating, as we cannot be with everyone, we castrate and put the animal up for adoption. If you are interested in adopting, consult the link "animals" and, schedule a visit through the form "contact us"

About Baby Shark Shelter

The baby shark shelter NGO was founded in 1996 by Mark and Lisie with its own resources.

Since there is no government assistance, the only source of funds is through donations.

We accept food, medicines and toys. If you can donate contact us by email babyhsharkshelter@hotmail.com and contact us.

If you want to work with us as a volunteer or even a veterinarian please contact us using the "work with us" form.
